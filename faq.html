FAQ

How do I add Chrxmaticc AI?

Click the invite button on the home page or use /invite in any server with the bot.

Is it free?

Yes. 100% free. No premium, no paywalls. Just chaos.

What AI model does it use?

Groq with multiple models — Llama 3.3 70B, GPT-OSS 120B, Qwen 32B, and more.

Does it store my messages?

Only what you explicitly set with /personalinfo. No spying, no selling data.

Can I customize how it looks?

Yes. Use /customize to change avatar, banner, bio, and nickname per server.

What are AI modes?

10 different personalities — Unfiltered, Insane, Comedian, Silent, Paranoid, Depressed, Disappointed, Teacher, Vibe, Suspicious.

How do I make the bot respond?

Use /chat to start a conversation, or @mention the bot with your message.

Custom commands?

Yes. Admins use /customcmd create to make server-specific commands.

How often does it update?

Constantly. Sometimes daily, sometimes weekly. Unlike some bots still on GPT 3.5.

What if it's offline?

Probably a quick restart for an update. It auto-recovers.
