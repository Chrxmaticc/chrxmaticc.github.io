Embed Builder

One box. Type anything. Pump it into /embed advanced and Chrxmaticc AI spits out your embed.

💡 Write your embed using key:value format below. Copy the whole thing, paste it into Discord with /embed advanced code: and the bot builds it instantly.

All Supported Keys

Every field Discord embeds support. Use any combination.

title:The title of the embed (max 256 chars)
desc:Description / main text (max 4096 chars)
color:Color name or hex — blue, red, green, purple, gold, pink, orange, cyan, #ff0000
author:Author name at the top
footer:Footer text at the bottom
image:URL to a large image (https only)
thumbnail:URL to a small icon image (https only)
timestamp:true or false — shows current time
